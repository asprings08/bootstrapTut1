Modular Scales

When it came to picking out modular scales for the typography hierarchies, I looked at Perfect Fourth, Major Second, and Major Third. For each hierarchy, Perfect Fourth looked too big. This modular scale seemed to need some larger visual element to balance out the high contrast between body and header text for the larger sized headings. On the other hand, Major Second felt too small. After h2 the headers seemed to blend right in with the body text. Major third became the default sweet spot that I chose to showcase each hierarchy for this assignment. The headers all stood out against the body text without becoming an overwhelming presence.
